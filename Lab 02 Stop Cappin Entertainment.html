Stop Cappin Entertainment.

Stop Cappin Entertainment Inc. is a nonprofit organization that creates entertaining charity events to spread awareness and raise money for charity. Our mission is to create events for teens and young adults to enjoy while also spreading awareness about charities or tragedies around the world. Through these events, our goal is to change the world for the better and show others that they can too.

History

Celebrity Charity Basketball Game

11/20/21

Stop Cappin Entertainment collaborated with 10 of the BEST content creator basketball players to present the Celebrity Charity Basketball Game. Through this event, Stop Cappin Entertainment was able to donate $250 worth of clothes and canned goods to Haiti during their time of crisis

Tiktok Vs Youtube Charity Kickball Game

02/19/22

A Tiktok Vs Youtube Kickball Match That Donated Over $200 Worth Of Food and Clothes to the Homeless People of Orange County. Team Youtube Took Home the Victory 11-1.

Tiktok Vs Youtube Charity Flag Football.

09/01/22

Evence Eliassaint and the members of Stop Cappin Ent. hosted a 7on7 “Tiktok Vs Youtube” Charity Flag Football Game in September of 2022 in Kissimmee Florida. This event matched up 7 tiktokers and 7 youtubers up against each other in a game of flag football in order to establish the better social media platform while also spreading suicide awareness during National Suicide Prevention Week.

Stop Cappin X BSU Celebrity Charity Basketball Game

02/24/23

Stop Cappin Entertainment partnered with the Black Student Union of the University of Central Florida to present the "Stop Cappin Entertainment X Black Student Union Celebrity Charity Basketball Game." The event had 250 total people in attendance and donated $500 to Elevate Orlando!
